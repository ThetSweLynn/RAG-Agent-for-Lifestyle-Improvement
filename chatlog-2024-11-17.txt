You: What is the role of hydration in athletic performance and recovery?
AI: Hydration plays a crucial role in athletic performance and recovery. Dehydration can lead to reduced endurance
and strength, and heat-related illness.  Matching fluid intake to fluid losses is advised, limiting
dehydration to no more than 2% of body weight loss.  Overhydration should also be avoided, especially in
events lasting longer than 4 hours.  During exercise lasting longer than 30 minutes, fluid intake is
beneficial. For intense exercise lasting over an hour, sports drinks are recommended over water due to their
sodium content, which promotes water retention and prevents hyponatremia.  For exercise under an hour, plain
water followed by a carbohydrate-rich snack within 2 hours is sufficient.  The ideal sports drink should
provide carbohydrates and electrolytes, prevent fluid imbalance, avoid gastrointestinal discomfort, and be
palatable.
